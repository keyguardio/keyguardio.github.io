Features

Sync

Passwords are auto-synced offline between devices. Internet not required.

AutoFill

Autofill passwords in Safari and native apps. No extra installation needed.

Share

Share a password with someone else. Uses E2EE and confirm codes.

Questions

  • How much does it cost?

    + -
    The app is free. We charge $ 2.99 per month if you want to use the Share feature.

  • Why is it offline?

    + -
    By keeping your passwords offline you never have to worry about anyone else getting access to your passwords.

  • How secure is it?

    + -
    Very secure. Apples crypto API's are used for everything security related. E2EE for sync. Encryption keys are stored in Apples Secure Enclave.

  • How do I get started?

    + -
    Download the app, link devices and setup AutoFill. You can also import passwords from other password managers.
